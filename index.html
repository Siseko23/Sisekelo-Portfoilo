In Development FEATURED CASE STUDY

FreightFlow Nexus

A B2B freight logistics platform for the South African market, covering the full booking lifecycle across four portals — shipper, carrier, driver and admin.

Problem

South African shippers juggle manual quote requests across carriers by phone, email and spreadsheet — no shared system for booking, live tracking, or proof of delivery, which bottlenecks dispatch and invoicing.

Approach

Built a four-portal platform on Flask and PostgreSQL, using Flask-SocketIO for real-time dispatch and GPS tracking, hosted on Azure. Covers instant multi-carrier quote comparison, dispatch assignment, live tracking, QR-based proof-of-delivery scanning, and invoicing triggered on delivery confirmation.

Where it stands

Core booking, quoting and tracking flows are built and running in development. Next milestones are carrier onboarding and the auto-invoicing module ahead of a pilot with SA freight operators.
